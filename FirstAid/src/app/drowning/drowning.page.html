Drowning

It should be remembered that a small child can drown in a few inches of water, even in the bathtub during the bath or in an almost empty tank.


What you should do:

  • Remove victim immediately from the water.
  • Check if you are conscious, if you breathe and if your heart beats.
  • Colocar a vítima de barriga para baixo e com a cabeça virada para um dos lados.
  • Compress the rib cage 3 to 4 times to get the water out.

If the victim does not breathe, lay him on his back and start artificial ventilation immediately by mouth-to-mouth and, if necessary, do cardiac massage.

As soon as the victim breathes normally, place it in a Safety Lateral Position and keep it comfortably warm.

In any situation, transport the victim to the Hospital.

What you should not do:

If the drowning occurred in the sea or in a river the rescuer should not:

  • Throw yourself in the water if you can not swim very well
  • Seeking to save a drowned man who is far from land
  • Let yourself be grabbed by the person you want to save

You should throw a rope or buoy.
