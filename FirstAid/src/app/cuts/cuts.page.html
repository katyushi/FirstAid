Cuts

Check the cut. If you can see fat, muscles or bones, you probably need stitches. Check the situation with a doctor if you are not sure. Signs of a problem that needs medical attention may include any of the following:

extreme pain ,

bleeding

and signs of shock (cold, excessive sweating,chills or pallor).

To know if a cut crossed the skin:
try to observe the presence of fat (irregular and yellow tissue), muscles (fibrous and red tissue) or bones (surface rigid and white).

Points are not necessary if the cut does not cross the skin. Treat the injury at home.
