Medicines
Signals and Symptons: Depend on the medicine ingested: one can observe vomiting. difficulty breathing. loss of consciousness. somnolence. mental confusion. etc.

What you should do:

Ask the victim to try to get as much information as possible about the poisoning.

Ask for directions to Poison control center immediately:

Indicate the product ingested, the probable amount, the time it was taken and the time of the last meal.

Keep the victim comfortably warm.

It is a serious situation that requires urgent transportation to the Hospital.
