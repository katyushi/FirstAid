Food products
Signals and Symptons:

Shivers and abundant perspiration.

abdominal pain.

nausea and vomiting.

diarrhea.

dizziness.

prostration.

syncope.

agitation and delirium.

What you should do:

Ask the victim to try to understand the source of the poisoning.

Keep the victim comfortably warm.

It is a serious situation that requires urgent transportation to the Hospital.
